What it supports

Designed for screening, triage, and transparent review.

Spatial exploration

Review mapped structure locations and public record context through a simple browser interface.

Priority signals

Surface records that may deserve follow-up based on reported-use patterns, available attributes, and review rules.

Plain-language context

Provide reasons and source links so reviewers can understand why a structure was surfaced.

Important limitation

Water decisions require authoritative records and professional judgment.

WaterGapMap helps organize information for review. It is not a substitute for agency determinations, legal analysis, engineering review, field verification, adjudication records, or direct consultation with qualified professionals.
