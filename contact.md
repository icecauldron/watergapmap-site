# Contact

WaterGapMap is an early-stage screening tool for Colorado water-data review workflows.

If you are a water professional, researcher, agency reviewer, engineer, attorney, consultant, ditch company representative, or interested user, feedback is welcome.

## What feedback is most useful?

The most helpful feedback focuses on:

* whether the review-priority methodology is directionally useful
* which assumptions or data limitations concern you most
* whether the map helps identify structures worth reviewing first
* what would increase confidence in the screening results
* what would make the tool more useful in real review workflows
